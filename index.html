The True Story Of Giant Company Wrigley

William Wrigley Jr.

The true story of the giant company Wrigley begins with the entrepreneurial spirit of a young man named William Wrigley Jr. Born in Philadelphia on September 30, 1861, Wrigley displayed an early aptitude for business and an unwavering determination that would eventually lead to the creation of one of the most iconic brands in the world.

In 1891, at the age of 29, Wrigley moved to Chicago and started his own soap and baking powder business. To differentiate his products, he began offering free chewing gum as an incentive with each purchase. Surprisingly, the gum became more popular than the products he was selling. Recognizing this, Wrigley shifted his focus entirely to the chewing gum market, a decision that would shape his legacy.

Wrigley established the Wm. Wrigley Jr. Company in 1891, laying the foundation for what would eventually become a multinational corporation. At the outset, the company produced various flavors of chewing gum, including Juicy Fruit and Spearmint, both of which would become synonymous with the Wrigley brand.

The turning point for the company came in 1915 when Wrigley revolutionized the industry by introducing a groundbreaking marketing strategy. He distributed free samples of chewing gum to every address listed in telephone directories across the United States. This bold move not only familiarized the public with his products but also solidified the Wrigley brand in the consciousness of American consumers.

With the success of his marketing endeavors, the Wrigley Company rapidly expanded its operations, establishing a global presence and dominating the chewing gum market. The company's commitment to innovation and high-quality products solidified its reputation as a leader in the confectionery industry.

Over the years, Wrigley's commitment to excellence and its relentless pursuit of customer satisfaction propelled the company to even greater heights. It became a subsidiary of Mars, Incorporated in 2008, further solidifying its position as a global powerhouse in the confectionery and food industry.

The legacy of Wrigley endures as a testament to the vision and perseverance of William Wrigley Jr. His commitment to providing high-quality, affordable products and his innovative marketing strategies continue to inspire businesses and entrepreneurs worldwide. The Wrigley Company remains a shining example of how a simple idea, driven by passion and perseverance, can evolve into a global empire.
